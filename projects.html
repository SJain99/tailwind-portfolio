Projects

Some of the projects I have, and continue to work on.

Epta

Status: Complete

Epta is a portfolio website that houses the portfolios of mine, and six of my other friends. The premise behind this is the fact that each of us brings a unique set of skills and knowledge to the table, that may be applicable to different jobs within a company. This way, if someone looks at one person's profile, they may be enticed to look at those of others too.

Tools used: Next.js (React.js), Chakra UI, Contentful (CMS)

Website available at: https://epta.ca

Spacestagram

Status: Complete

This website was made as part of the Shopify Frontend Developer Internship application for Summer 2022. The premise behind it was to build a social media website to showcase NASA's Astronomy Picture of the Day and serve as the "image-sharing website from the final frontier". My inspiration for this website was a combination of Instagram for the image layout, and Facebook for the UI surrounding the image.

Tools used: Next.js (React.js), Chakra UI

Website available at: https://spacestagram-lovat.vercel.app/

ShutterShrike

Status: In Progress

ShutterShrike aims to be a peer-to-peer marketplace for photography and videography equipment. The idea behind this startup concept is twofold: one is to allow smaller content creators to rent the equipment they may require for their projects for a lower cost than buying the equipment outright, as well as for those who own equipment that they do not actively use to make some money with it through renting it out.

Tools currently being used: Next.js (React.js), MySQL, Tailwind CSS

Current build available at: https://shuttershrike.ca
